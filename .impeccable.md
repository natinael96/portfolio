# Design Context

## Target audience

Hiring managers, technical leads, and recruiters evaluating backend and full-stack engineering capability. Secondary: peers and collaborators browsing for collaboration.

## Use cases

Skim credibility and stack fit, open projects or GitHub, and initiate contact (form or email). Fast scan on mobile and focused read on desktop.

## Brand personality / tone

Calm, precise, and editorial—confident engineering craft without startup clichés. Light, warm neutrals with strong typographic hierarchy; motion is subtle and structural (never gimmicky).
